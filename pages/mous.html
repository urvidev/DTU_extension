Memorandum of Understanding (MoUs)

Strategic partnerships for community impact

Our Strategic Partners

DTU Extension & Outreach Cell collaborates with esteemed organizations to maximize our social impact and reach. These partnerships help us create sustainable and scalable community development programs.

M/s Paytm Foundation

MoU Signed: January 15, 2022 Duration: 5 Years

The partnership between DTU Extension & Outreach Cell and Paytm Foundation focuses on leveraging technology to enhance digital literacy among underprivileged communities. This collaboration aims to bridge the digital divide and provide skills that increase employability in the modern workforce.

Key Areas of Collaboration

  • Digital Literacy Programs in underserved areas
  • Fintech education and awareness workshops
  • Technology infrastructure support for educational initiatives
  • Student internship opportunities with Paytm
  • Joint research on financial inclusion technologies
Recent Initiatives
Mobile App Development Workshop

A three-month program teaching mobile application development to 100+ students from rural areas around Delhi

Financial Literacy Program

Series of workshops on digital payments, online banking, and cyber security for senior citizens and small business owners

Impact
Year People Reached Communities Served Programs Conducted
2022 1,500+ 8 12
2023 2,800+ 15 20
Future Plans

The partnership aims to expand its reach to 20+ communities in the next two years, with special focus on women's empowerment through technology and entrepreneurship training. A joint innovation lab is also being planned to develop low-cost technology solutions for rural areas.

M/s Sudha Kotwal Educational Charitable Trust

MoU Signed: March 20, 2022 Duration: 3 Years

The collaboration between DTU Extension & Outreach Cell and Sudha Kotwal Educational Charitable Trust is focused on enhancing educational opportunities for underprivileged children, with special emphasis on girls' education and vocational training for sustainable livelihoods.

Key Areas of Collaboration

  • Scholarship programs for underprivileged students
  • Women's education and empowerment initiatives
  • Vocational training centers in rural areas
  • Educational infrastructure development
  • Joint community outreach programs
Recent Initiatives
Scholarship Program

50 full scholarships provided to talented students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds to pursue higher education

Vocational Training Center

Establishment of a vocational training center in rural Delhi providing skills in tailoring, beauty care, and computer operations

Impact
Initiative Beneficiaries Locations
Scholarship Program 50 students 5 villages around Delhi
Vocational Training 200+ women 3 centers
Education Camps 500+ children 12 communities
Future Plans

The partnership plans to establish a model school in a rural area that will provide quality education with modern facilities to underprivileged children. Additionally, the collaboration aims to expand the scholarship program to cover 100 students annually and establish digital libraries in remote areas.
