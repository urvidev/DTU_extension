Education Initiatives

Empowering students through skill enhancement programs

FEC-6

Foundation Engineering Course (FEC-6)

A specialized skill enhancement program designed for 1st year DTU students to build a strong foundation in engineering principles and technical skills.

  • Hands-on technical training
  • Expert-led workshops
  • Engineering fundamentals
View Gallery

Course Details

The Foundation Engineering Course (FEC-6) is a comprehensive program that introduces first-year students to key engineering concepts and practical applications. The course consists of multiple modules covering various engineering disciplines.

Course Structure:
Module Duration Topics Covered
Module 1 2 weeks Introduction to Engineering Principles
Module 2 3 weeks Technical Drawing and Design
Module 3 3 weeks Practical Applications and Projects

Community Outreach

Extending education and support to communities beyond campus

Featured

Lab on Wheels (LOW)

A mobile education initiative that brings technological learning to underserved communities. This innovative program features a mobile computer lab that travels to schools and communities to provide hands-on technology education.

Basic Computer Course (BCC)

Introductory computer literacy program teaching fundamental skills including typing, file management, internet usage, and basic office applications.

Basic Python Programming (BPP)

Entry-level programming course introducing participants to coding concepts through Python, focusing on problem-solving and computational thinking.

View Gallery

Lab on Wheels Details

The Lab on Wheels initiative was launched to bridge the digital divide by bringing technology education directly to communities that lack access to computer facilities. The mobile lab is equipped with laptops, internet connectivity, and educational materials.

Basic Computer Course (BCC)
  • Introduction to computers and operating systems
  • Basic typing and document creation skills
  • Internet browsing and online safety
  • Email and communication tools
  • Introduction to office applications
Basic Python Programming (BPP)
  • Introduction to programming concepts
  • Python syntax and basic data types
  • Conditional statements and loops
  • Functions and basic programs
  • Simple problem-solving applications

UBA

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA)

A flagship program led by DTU to bring transformational change in rural development processes by leveraging knowledge institutions to help build the architecture of an Inclusive India.

  • Rural development initiatives
  • Technology intervention for village problems
  • Community awareness programs
  • Student engagement in social responsibility
View Gallery

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan Details

DTU is actively participating in the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan program initiated by the Ministry of Education. Through this program, DTU has adopted five villages to implement development projects and provide technological solutions to rural challenges.

Adopted Villages:
Village Name Focus Areas Key Initiatives
Village 1 Water Conservation Rainwater harvesting systems, Water quality testing
Village 2 Renewable Energy Solar lighting, Biogas implementation
Village 3 Education Digital literacy program, School infrastructure improvement
Village 4 Sanitation Toilet construction, Waste management solutions
Village 5 Agriculture Irrigation technology, Crop management training

Annual & Recurring Activities

Regular events that engage and inspire our community

Exploring Engineering

Summer Vacation
2-Week Program

Program Details

The Exploring Engineering program gives school students a taste of different engineering disciplines through engaging activities and projects supervised by DTU faculty and students.

Program Schedule:

  • Week 2: Hands-on Projects and Engineering Challenges
