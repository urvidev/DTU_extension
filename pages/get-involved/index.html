Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about volunteering with us

We offer flexible volunteering options that can fit various schedules. You can choose to volunteer for a few hours per week or month, depending on your availability and the specific program you're interested in.

Requirements vary depending on the role. For mentoring positions, relevant experience or expertise is valuable. For general volunteering, enthusiasm and commitment are the most important qualifications. All volunteers receive orientation and training.

Partner organizations receive support including program design assistance, training for staff, access to educational resources, and coordination support for implementation. We work collaboratively to ensure programs are successful and sustainable.
